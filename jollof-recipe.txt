Ingredients:
- some rice
- stew base
- mixed vegetables
- onions
- tomato paste
- broth
- powdered seasoning
- salt
- any other spice preferences

Directions:
1. Boil the stew base with the tomato paste
2. Season the stew base with the powdered seasoning and salt
3. Add the broth
4. Wash an parboil the rice with the broth
5. Pour the parboiled rice into the cooking stew base and cover the pot
6. Add the onions and the mixed vegetables
